Our Mission

The biggest problem beginners have when looking to explore backpacking and hiking is knowing where to start. The mission Adventure Expeditions has is to provide a website that explores what it takes to backpack on an expedition successfully. We provide important suggestions on how to plan for an expedition, what types of equipment to include in your packing, and information on popular expedition locations.
